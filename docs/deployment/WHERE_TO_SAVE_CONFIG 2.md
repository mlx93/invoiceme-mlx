# Where to Find "Save Configuration" Button

**Location**: Top right of the Configuration page

---

## Method 1: Save from Configuration Page (Easiest)

1. **You're already on the Configuration page** ✅
2. **Look at the TOP RIGHT** of the page (above the configuration sections)
3. **Look for**: 
   - "Save configuration" button (might be next to "Upload and deploy")
   - OR in an "Actions" dropdown menu
   - OR as a button near "Cancel" / "Apply changes"

**If you don't see it**, try:

### Alternative: Save from Application Level

1. **Click "Applications"** in the left sidebar (top of sidebar)
2. **Click on**: `invoiceme-mlx-backend` (your application)
3. **Click "Saved configurations"** (left sidebar, under the application)
4. **Click "Create saved configuration"** button
5. **Select source**: Choose `Invoiceme-mlx-backend-env` (your environment)
6. **Name**: `invoiceme-backend-config`
7. **Description**: "InvoiceMe backend with all environment variables"
8. **Click "Create"**

---

## Method 2: Check Actions Menu

1. **Look for "Actions" dropdown** (top right of Configuration page)
2. **Click it**
3. **Look for**: "Save configuration" option

---

## Method 3: From Environment Overview

1. **Go to "Overview"** (left sidebar, under your environment)
2. **Look for "Actions" dropdown** (top right)
3. **Click it** → Look for "Save configuration"

---

## What You Should See

After saving, you can verify:

1. **Go to Application**: `invoiceme-mlx-backend`
2. **Click "Saved configurations"** (left sidebar)
3. **You should see**: `invoiceme-backend-config` listed

---

## Quick Check: Look at Top Right

On your Configuration page, look at the **very top right corner**:

- Do you see buttons like: "Cancel", "Review changes", "Apply changes"?
- Is there an "Actions" dropdown nearby?
- Is there a "Save configuration" button?

**If you can't find it**, use Method 1 (Application level) - that's the most reliable way!

---

**Next Step**: Try the Application level method (Method 1 Alternative) - it's the most straightforward!
